Chocolava Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate (roughly chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Butter (unsalted)
  • 1/4 cup All-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup Powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • A pinch of Salt
  • Butter (for greasing the molds)
  • Cocoa powder or flour (for dusting the molds)

Instructions

    1.Prepare the Molds:
  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Grease your ramekins or muffin molds with butter and dust them with cocoa powder or flour to prevent sticking.
    3. 2.Melt Chocolate and Butter:
  3. In a heatproof bowl, melt the chopped dark chocolate and butter together. You can do this using a double boiler or in the microwave in 20-second intervals, stirring in between. Mix until smooth, and set aside to cool slightly.
    4. 3.Prepare the Batter:
  4. In another bowl, whisk the eggs and powdered sugar together until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  5. Gradually add the melted chocolate-butter mixture and mix well.
  6. Sift in the flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Fold gently until everything is well combined.
  7. Add the vanilla extract and stir.
    8. 4.Fill the Molds:
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared molds, filling them about 3/4 of the way.
    9. 5.Bake:
  9. Bake the choco lava cakes in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes. The edges should be set, but the center should still be soft to create the lava effect.
    10. 6.Serve:
  10. Remove from the oven and let the cakes cool for about 1-2 minutes.
  11. Gently run a knife around the edges of the molds and carefully invert the cakes onto serving plates.

INDIAN THALI RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • Oil for cooking
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 inch piece ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, mince
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 cup paneer cubes

Instructions

    1.Cook the rice:
  1. Cook the basmati rice according to package instructions.
    2. 2.Make the paneer curry:
  2. In a pot, heat oil. Add onion, garlic, ginger, and spices. Add paneer cubes and simmer until heated through
    3. 3.Sauté spinach:
  3. In another pan, sauté spinach leaves until wilted.
    4. 4.Assemble the thali:
  4. Place a portion of rice on each plate. Arrange the paneer curry, sautéed spinach, raita, and papad around the rice.

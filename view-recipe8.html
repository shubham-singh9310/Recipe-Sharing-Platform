Idli Sambhar Recipe

Idli Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cup Urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups Idli Rava (rice rava) or 2 cups Rice (if using rice, soak and grind)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for soaking and grinding
  • Oil for greasing idli molds

Instructions

    1.Soak the Dal and Rice:
  1. Soak urad dal and idli rava (or rice) in water separately for at least 4-5 hours or overnight.
    2. 2.Grind the Batter:
  2. Drain the water from the urad dal and grind it into a smooth, fluffy batter by adding water little by little.
  3. If using rice, grind the soaked rice into a coarse paste (for a grainy texture). If using idli rava, just drain the water and mix it directly with the urad dal batter.
    4. 3.Fermentation:
  4. Mix the urad dal batter and the ground rice (or idli rava) together. Add salt and mix well.
  5. Cover the batter and let it ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or overnight. The batter should double in volume.
    6. 3.Steam the Idlis:
  6. Once the batter is fermented, gently stir it.
  7. Grease idli molds with oil and pour the batter into them.
  8. Steam the idlis for 10-15 minutes in a steamer or pressure cooker (without the whistle) until they are fluffy and cooked through.
    9. 4.Serve:
  9. Let the idlis cool for a minute before removing them from the molds. Serve hot with sambhar and coconut chutney.

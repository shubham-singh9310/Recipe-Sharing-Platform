CHILLI PANEER RECIP

Ingredients

  • 250 grams Paneer (cubed)
  • 1 tablespoon Cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon Soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon Red chili sauce
  • 1 Onion (cubed)
  • 1 Capsicum (cubed)
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions

    1.Fry the Paneer:
  1. Toss paneer cubes in cornflour and a pinch of salt.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, and lightly fry the paneer until golden. Set aside.
    3. 2.Sauté Vegetables:
  3. In the same pan, add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add cubed onion and capsicum, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes on medium-high heat.
    5. 3.Prepare Sauce:
  5. Add soy sauce, red chili sauce, and tomato ketchup. Stir well.
  6. Add fried paneer cubes, toss everything together, and cook for 2 more minutes. Adjust salt and pepper.
    7. 4.Garnish
  7. Garnish with spring onions if desired.

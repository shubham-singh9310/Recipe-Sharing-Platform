CHICKEN CURRY RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

    1.Heat&cook
  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
    2. 2.sauté vegetables
  2. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook until the onion is softened, about 5 minutes.
    3. 3.Cook
  3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
    4. 4.Boiling
  4. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
    5. 5.Salt
  5. Season with salt to taste.
    6. 6.Serve
  6. Serve hot with rice or naan bread.

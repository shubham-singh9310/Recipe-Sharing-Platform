CHICKEN BIRYANI RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 kg basmati rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic,
  • 1 inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or butter for layering

Instructions

    1.Cook the rice:
  1. Cook the soaked basmati rice according to package instructions.
    2. 2.Prepare the chicken:
  2. Heat ghee or butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
    3. 3.Make the masala:
  3. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger to the pot and cook until softened. Stir in the diced tomatoes, cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
    4. 4.Combine rice and masala:
  4. Add the cooked rice to the pot and gently mix with the masala.
    5. 5.Layer the biryani:
  5. Transfer the rice and masala mixture to a biryani pot or a large baking dish. Layer with ghee or butter, and sprinkle saffron strands on top (optional).
    6. 6.Cook:
  6. Cover the pot and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the flavors have blended.
    7. 7.Let it rest:
  7. Let the biryani rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
    8. Serve hot:
  8. Serve the biryani with raita (yogurt-based condiment) and your favorite side dishes.

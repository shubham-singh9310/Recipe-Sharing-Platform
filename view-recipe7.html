Veg Manchurian Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cabbage, finely grated
  • 1 cup Carrot, finely grated
  • 1/2 cup Capsicum (bell pepper), finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Spring onions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons All-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger, finely chopped
  • 1-2 Green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Spring onions, chopped (white and green parts separated)
  • 2 tablespoons Soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Red chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon Vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoons water (cornstarch slurry)
  • 1/4 cup Water (adjust for consistency)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sugar (optional)
  • Fresh coriander or spring onion greens for garnish

Instructions

    1.Prepare the Veggie Mixture:
  1. In a large bowl, combine grated cabbage, carrot, chopped capsicum, and spring onions.
  2. Add cornflour, all-purpose flour, ginger-garlic paste, black pepper powder, and salt. Mix everything well.
  3. Squeeze out any excess water from the vegetables. The flour should be enough to bind the veggies together to form balls. If the mixture is too wet, add a little more cornflour.
    4. 2.Shape and Fry:
  4. Take small portions of the mixture and shape them into round balls.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan or wok. Once hot, deep-fry the veg balls in batches until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
    6. 3.Sauté Aromatics:
  6. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a wok or large pan. Add finely chopped garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
    7. 4.Add Veggies:
  7. Add the white parts of the spring onions and sauté for another minute until slightly softened.
    8. 5.Add Sauces:
  8. Add soy sauce, red chili sauce, and tomato ketchup. Stir well to combine.
    9. 6.Add Cornstarch Slurry:
  9. Add the cornstarch slurry and water. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens slightly.
    10. 7.Season:
  10. Add salt, vinegar, and sugar (if using) to balance the flavors. Simmer the sauce for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
    11. 8.Mix the Veg Balls with Sauce:
  11. Once the sauce reaches the desired consistency, add the fried veg balls to the sauce and gently toss to coat them evenly.
    12. 9.Garnish and Serve:
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander or spring onion greens and serve hot.
